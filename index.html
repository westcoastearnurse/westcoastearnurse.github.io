Services

The West Coast Ear Nurse Clinic provides a friendly, knowledgeable, professional service for all ages. We conduct clinics from Karamea to Haast,  we value equal access for everyone for ear treatment! We can also come to you. Inquire for home, residential, work place visits (at extra cost).

ACC funded visits

War veteran's free

Work & Income payment cards accepted.

(Conditions apply)

Wax removal by micro-suction/probe: Using best practice techniques the wax is safely removed by micro-suction, under a magnified head scope.


Removal of foreign bodies: When an object is stuck or placed in your ear it is known as a foreign body. Once the object is identified by the clinician it can be safely removed by micro-suction & special ear instruments.

Assessment & Management of common ear problems: Ear wax build up, outer ear infection (otitis externa), middle ear infection (otitis media). Ear canal dermatitis/eczema. Surfer's ear (Exostoses).

Education & Advice: Informative and practical advice to meet your education needs. For instance, if u use cotton buds in your ears it will be explained to you why your being told off in a nice way!

Referrals: Can be placed to other specialists as needed. (Otolarygologist, Audiologist). May incur an extra cost.
